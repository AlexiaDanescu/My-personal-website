Hello!

My name is Dănescu Alexia Loredana and I am a first year student at the Faculty of Electronics, Telecommunications and Information Technology from Technical University of Cluj-Napoca.

I was born and raised in Targoviste, Dambovita, but I came to study in Cluj-Napoca because I was atracted by the beautiful architecture and Transylvania’ s lifestyle, but mostly because of the warmth and kindness of people.

I am a dynamic and very curious person so I am enthusiastic about learning new things. From the beginning of the academic year I didn’t miss an opportunity to get involved in various activities. For example, I went to multiple companies’ s workshops and presentations, I participated in hackathons and I am currently participating in a RADIO-ASTRONOMY course. Also, in the first semester I was selected to participate in a course, where I learned with 29 participants the basics of programming in PYTHON. The trainer was a professor from my University and the course was supported by Q_Perior. Recently, I started learning from totorials from the Internet C# and how to use UNITY to make my own game. My personal WEB page was also made by me, using HTML, CSS, and php. For hosting I used GITHUB.

I am currently a member of ROBOTELIER, which is a team of students are passionate about robotics. From them I learned using ARDUINO and then I started making little projects. Our team recently organized ELECTROCAMP, in collaboration with Faculty of Electrical Engineering. It’s a 2-day Arduino training for high school students, followed by a contest in which they had to make a line-folower, based on what they learned. I participated there as VOLUNTEER and gave advice to anyone who needed help. I won’t miss any chance to participate again in the future editions or in any similar events where my help is needed .
